Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

1. Overview

Damascus Maker is a crafting/design utility for creating and previewing Damascus-style pattern recipes. The app is designed to work offline. It does not require an account, does not include advertising, and does not transmit your recipes or inputs to external servers.

2. Information Collection

The app does not collect personal information, contact details, device identifiers, or usage analytics for the developer. The app does not request access to your location, photos, microphone, camera, contacts, or Bluetooth.

3. Tracking

The app does not track you across apps and websites, and it does not use advertising SDKs.

4. On-Device Storage

The app may store your settings and/or saved recipes locally on your device so they remain available between launches. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted by the app.

Note: if you enable iOS device backups (e.g., iCloud Backup or encrypted Finder/iTunes backup), Apple may include app data in those backups. The developer does not receive that data.

5. Third-Party Access

The app does not embed third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking libraries, and it does not share your data with third parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Because it does not collect data, it is suitable for all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If the app’s functionality changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated with a new effective date.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or support, email wangning.engineer@outlook.com.
