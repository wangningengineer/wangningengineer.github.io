App Support Center

Overview

Damascus Maker helps you create and preview Damascus-style pattern recipes. The app is designed to work offline. It does not require an account, does not include ads, and does not transmit your recipes or inputs to any server.

Key Facts (for App Review)

  • Offline only: no cloud sync and no server connections are required for core features.
  • No data collection: the app does not collect personal information, identifiers, or usage analytics for the developer.
  • No tracking: the app does not use advertising or tracking SDKs.
  • Local storage: your settings and/or saved recipes (if any) are stored locally on your device.

FAQ

  • Does the app need the internet? No. The app works offline.
  • Does the app collect data? No. The app does not collect or transmit your personal data.
  • Does the app store anything? Saved settings/recipes (if you choose to save) stay on your device.
